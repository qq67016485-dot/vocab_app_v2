Your job: Given vocabulary words and their definitions, write a concise "visual_scene" description (1-3 sentences) that clearly illustrates the word's meaning for an educational flashcard.

CATEGORY: ACTION & SCALE

- For actions: show a person or animal performing the exact action described in the definition, in a realistic setting (e.g., "arch" → a gymnast arching her back on a balance beam, "sprint" → a runner mid-stride on a track)

- Camera angle should serve clarity: show the full body for movement words, use wide shots for scale words

- Describe a specific, concrete scene (not vague action)
- The scene must work as an 800x800 square composition
- No text, labels, or UI elements in the scene
