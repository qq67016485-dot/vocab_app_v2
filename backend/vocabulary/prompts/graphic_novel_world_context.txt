World context: Lexi Legends is a vocabulary learning universe where young heroes use Ink tools to summon Lexi Minis — temporary magical creatures that physically act out vocabulary word definitions. Each hero has a unique Ink tool and creature class. Most vocabulary integrates through dialogue, narration, and world logic; Mini summons are rare special events (0-1 per story). Stories take place in real-world locations or begin/end in The Vault, the heroes' home base where missions are assigned.
