SETTING: The Vault Reading Stacks, a discovery wall inside the same rounded Vault dome. Smooth teal-and-off-white archive wall modules with inset rounded-rectangle story tile bays, child-eye-level tabs, warm vertical light strips, thin warm-gold catalog lines, and one active story tile, file, book, card, or tab glowing softly.

FUNCTION: Story-object selection and clue discovery. One readable title, clue, missing word, or tab is the focal text. Distant tiles simplify into clean shapes, not dense text.

COMPOSITION: Hero or Folio at child scale near the active tile. Use the archive wall, catalog lines, and character gaze to guide the eye to one focal object. Keep broad quiet surfaces and readable silhouettes.

MATERIALS / LIGHT: Matte deep teal ceramic bands, warm off-white inset panels, satin brass trim, warm-gold catalog glow, sparse paper texture only on the active story object.

DO NOT: dense shelf forests, piles of books, rolling ladders, carts, old-library furniture, tiny fake text, classroom or museum display, more than one active story object.
