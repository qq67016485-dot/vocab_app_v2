# Mei (9 years old) Visual Sheet

Runtime use: script generation and image generation
Age: 9 years old
Payload rule: load this entire sheet when Mei (9 years old) appears in a story

## Core Identity

Mei is the quick-footed action kid of Lexi Legends: fast in timing and reactions, not superpowered speed; alert, funny, competitive in a friendly way, learning that the sharpest move is sometimes a pause, a signal, or matching the pace of the moment.

Visual signature: **white polo with dark green trim + white pleated tennis skirt + headphones around neck + glowing multicolor marker + high side ponytail + Agile Lexi Minis**.

Visual grounding: Mei is a modern 9-year-old sporty Asian kid using a magical multicolor marker for word-art, not a superhero, ninja, racer mascot, or sci-fi soldier. The polo, skirt, and marker should feel like bright graphic-novel learning gear over everyday athletic clothes.

## Story Role & Personality

The timing-and-motion character. Use her when vocabulary must be proven through timing, sequence, direction, changing conditions, or helping others keep pace. Fast rather than frantic: her speed creates openings, not chaos.

Energetic, observant, playful, and decisive. She likes a challenge and cheers when someone else gets one right. Comedy comes from her impatient timing, quick sound effects, and competitive grin, not from making her reckless or rude.

Under pressure: bounces once on the balls of her feet, checks the path, then moves. She can rush, skid, need to reset, or admit she missed a detail; confidence is her tendency, not a permanent state. If wrong, circles back fast and tries the cleaner move. Never mocks someone who moves or thinks more slowly.

Bravery: moves first when someone needs help, but learns to glance back and make sure the path is readable for others.

Flaw: sometimes treats the first clue like a starting whistle, but she is learning that waiting or choosing the slower route can make the next move sharper.

Emotional range: frustrated — taps the marker cap against her palm in a quick rhythm. Excited — rocks forward with a bright grin and clipped sentences. Disappointed — plants both feet, exhales hard, then looks for the next opening.

## Dialogue Voice

Crisp, active lines with verbs, timing words, and challenge energy. Bright 9-year-old: direct, playful, quick to react, not polished.

Sample phrases (inspiration, not mandatory):
- "Wait-wait-now."
- "Too slow? Or too soon?"
- "That word took the corner too wide."
- "My feet got ahead of my brain."
- "I can hit that beat."

## Physical Design

Age 9, about 135 cm. Slim compact athletic build, springy and energetic — can stop, pivot, or sprint in a small space.
Skin: fair with warm peachy undertones.
Face: round heart-shaped, full rosy cheeks, small pointed chin, small delicate nose. Large warm brown round eyes with a bright sparkling look. Soft arched dark brown eyebrows. Smile is quick, wide, and friendly.
Hair: dark brown (near-black), thick and slightly wavy with natural soft flyaways. Styled in a single high side ponytail tied on Mei's left (viewer's right in front view) with a thin red elastic hair tie, ~24 cm from tie to upper back with bouncy swing. Short loose bangs sweep right, never covering eyes, with a few strands framing the face.
Proportions: head-to-body ratio ~1:5.6, limbs childlike, not teen-long. Soft unsharpened facial features, no makeup.
Posture: forward-ready, knees slightly bent, weight on balls of feet. Holds the glowing multicolor marker in her writing hand near chest level.

## Outfit

- Polo shirt: white short-sleeved polo shirt with dark forest green trim. Contrasting dark green ribbed collar with a single white button placket at the neck. Dark green cuffs on the sleeves, with two parallel dark green stripes running down the outer side of each sleeve.
- Skirt: white A-line pleated tennis skirt, above knee length, with a subtle dark green accent stripe on the left side (visible in profile view). Crisp, evenly spaced pleats.
- Socks: plain white crew socks, reaching mid-calf.
- Shoes: white low-top athletic sneakers with dark green stripes on the outer sides. Classic clean tennis shoe design with white laces and white rubber soles.
- Headphones: white over-ear headphones with dark green accents on the ear cups. Default position is around her neck. May be worn over ears when listening or concentrating.
- No excessive accessories; preserve the clean sporty silhouette.

## Glowing Multicolor Marker

One glowing multicolor marker, ~12 cm long, chunky school-marker shape with a rounded pink cap and blunt felt tip. Body features a bright neon gradient (yellow, green, pink, purple). Tip glows with shifting neon colors when writing. Emits clean, non-toxic Ink light that draws readable words, arrows, beats, and motion paths. Writing tool, not a weapon. Default: held in writing hand at chest level.

Mei's Ink summons Lexi Minis when she draws a vocabulary word. The multicolor marker only appears on-page during a Lexi Mini summon (0-2 per story). In panels without a Mini summon, Mei does not hold or display the marker.

## Lexi Mini: The Agile

When Mei draws a vocabulary word with her multicolor marker, it hatches into a Lexi Mini — a temporary magical creature made of shifting neon speed-lines with motion-blur trails. Mei's Minis are kinetic, energetic, and fast.

Creature types: kitsunes (foxes), jackalopes, winged monkeys, lightning-wyverns, swift-stags.

Nature: kinetic, routing, sequencing, and energetic. They vibrate with kinetic energy.

Problem-solving style: fetches, connects, scouts, outpaces, or physically organizes things in rapid sequence. The Mini demonstrates the target word's meaning through quick rhythmic action to solve the story problem, then dissolves back into Ink.

Visuals: made of shifting neon (yellow/green/pink/purple) speed-lines with motion-blur trails. Multicolor glow extends softly with short afterimages. The Mini must be clearly visible and distinct from Mei herself.

Example: Mei draws "SWIFT" → a neon fox forms from the marker arcs, dashes along the stuck gear in a blur of color, setting it spinning freely.

## Realm Adaptation

Mei can enter any Story Realm. Temporary accessories (rain cover, safety pads, badge, scarf, relay sash, etc.) are fine as long as the white polo with green trim remains visible and the glowing multicolor marker is not replaced. The signature tool is for Lexi Mini summons only. Characters may freely interact with world objects (keys, ropes, tools, maps, sports gear, etc.).

## Do Not

- Change the white polo + dark green trim or cover it for most of a story.
- Change the multicolor Ink to a single flat color.
- Replace the glowing multicolor marker with another tool.
- Show the marker actively writing in panels without a Lexi Mini summon.
- Make her a superhero, ninja, soldier, racer mascot, or sci-fi combat character.
- Give her armor, combat blades, gun-like marker props, or a full-face tech HUD.
- Make her mean, dismissive, reckless, or obsessed with winning.
- Make target vocabulary words unreadable, tiny, or overly stylized.
- Add real-world brand logos.
- Keep her visual age clearly 9 years old; never make her adult-proportioned.
- Make her narrate her own learning explicitly (e.g., "I learned that X means Y!").
